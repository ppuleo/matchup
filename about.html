About

When I moved to San Francisco a few months ago, I stayed in a Travelodge for a few weeks while I looked for a reasonable apartment in the city (Pro tip: there's no such thing). With spotty Wi-Fi and only 3 TV channels, I was understandably bored out of my mind.

In the evenings, when I wasn't watching Telemundo with the sound off, I would entertain myself by making satirical bingo cards for Travelodge. "Door Slam!" and "Suspicious Stains" were common to every room. "Shower Temperature Roulette", "Loud Neighbor Sex", and "Bed Bugs" were rarer treats.

To commemorate that awful stay, I made this memory matching game with all of my least favorite squares in Travelodge bingo. Give it a try or go stay in a Travelodge to play for real.

Technology

Travelodge Match-Up is built on bootstrap and uses some fancy new CSS3 transforms and transitions to flip the cards (unless you're using IE - then no flips for you). For more info on the CSS, check out Chris Ruppel's excellent examples at CSS3 Playground. Also, you can cheat by clicking extra fast.
